Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when using our services:

  • Name, email address, phone number when you contact us or register
  • School or organisation details for product demonstrations
  • Usage data, device information, and log files when using our platforms
  • Location data (only with explicit permission) for GPS-enabled features

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information to:

  • Provide, maintain and improve our products and services
  • Send service updates, support messages, and account notifications
  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers in India. We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256 at rest, TLS in transit), access controls, and regular security audits. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We share your data only with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our platform (e.g., cloud hosting, payment processors)
  • Government or law enforcement agencies when required by Indian law
  • Other parties only with your explicit consent

5. Student Data

Student data collected through our School ERP platform is owned by the school institution. We process this data only as a data processor on behalf of the school. Student data is never used for advertising purposes.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. Analytics cookies (if used) can be disabled through your browser settings. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@trivarta.in. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Upon account deletion, personal data is deleted within 90 days unless required by law.

9. Children's Privacy

Our platforms for students are used under the supervision and authority of school institutions. We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children under 13 without parental/school consent.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related queries: privacy@trivarta.in
