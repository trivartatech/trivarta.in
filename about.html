Our Story

How We Got Here

2018

The Beginning

Trivarta Tech was founded by a small team of software engineers frustrated by clunky, expensive school management systems. We built our first ERP for a single school in 90 days.

2020

Going Mobile

We launched Android and iOS apps for parents and students. Real-time GPS tracking and push notifications became our standout features during the pandemic-driven digital shift.

2022

AI & Expansion

We integrated an AI grading engine and behaviour analytics. Our client base grew to 80+ schools across three states.

2024 →

Full Platform

Today we serve 150+ schools and 50,000+ students. Our platform includes 22+ modules, Razorpay fee payments, Firebase notifications, and a growing AI engine.
