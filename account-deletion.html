Account Deletion

Request deletion of your Schools 1 account and all associated personal data

1. Introduction

At Trivarta Tech Pvt Ltd, we respect your right to control your personal data. You can request the deletion of your Schools 1 account and all associated personal data at any time. This page explains how to submit a deletion request and what happens to your data after deletion.

2. How to Request Account Deletion

Email Request

Send an email to our support team with your deletion request:

trivarta.tech@gmail.com

Required Information:

  • Your registered email address
  • Account username or user ID
  • School name (if applicable)
  • Any additional details to help identify your account

3. What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon successful account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

User profile information
Login credentials
Email address
Phone number
School association data
App usage data linked to account

4. What Data May Be Retained

Important Notice

In certain circumstances, we may be required to retain some data for:

  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal proceedings
  • Fraud Prevention: To prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues
  • Security Purposes: To protect our services and users

Any retained data will be limited to the minimum required and will be deleted as soon as the retention purpose is fulfilled.

5. Deletion Processing Time

7 Business Days

Your account deletion request will be processed and completed within 7 business days from the date we receive and verify your request.

6. Confirmation

Deletion Confirmation

Once your account and associated data have been successfully deleted, you will receive a confirmation email at your registered email address. This email will confirm that your deletion request has been completed.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions about the account deletion process or need assistance, please contact us:
