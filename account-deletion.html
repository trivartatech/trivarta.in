Account Deletion Request

We respect your right to remove your data from our systems.

Important: If you are a school admin, account deletion must be requested through the school's designated administrator. Individual teacher or student accounts can only be deleted by the school admin.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your profile and login credentials
  • Personal contact information
  • App preferences and settings
  • Notification history

What May Be Retained

  • Academic records (owned by school)
  • Fee transaction records (legal requirement)
  • Audit logs (regulatory compliance)
  • Anonymised usage data

Request Account Deletion

Fill in the form below. We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm via email.

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you have downloaded any data you need before submitting.

Alternatively, email us at contactus@trivarta.in or WhatsApp us with your registered details.
